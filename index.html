Energy Efficiency Calculator

Introduction

This application aims to bring advocacy to the public about renewable energy, namely solar. It comprises of an application that analyses kitchen appliances using the model number, as the kitchen is the most energy consuming room in a household. It display information about the appliance along and also shows how much you would be saving by using a solar panel.

Instructions

Scroll down or clock on the Efficiency Caculator button at the top of the webpage to take you to the search area. Enter your Model Number of your kitchen appliance to the search area and press 'Find Appliance' for your appliance's information to be displayed on the page.
